Our Story

Built in Southern Ocean County, for Southern Ocean County

For over 30 years, Sheridan Heating & Cooling has delivered the kind of service Ocean County homeowners can count on — reliable, knowledgeable, and done right the first time.

We've worked across Ocean County for over 30 years — new construction, aging systems, and everything in between. When Hurricane Sandy hit the Jersey Shore, we worked extremely hard to get people comfortable again.

We don't advertise much. We don't need to. Our work speaks for itself, and our customers speak for us — in 5-star Google reviews, Nextdoor recommendations, and word of mouth that has carried us for three decades.
