How do I know if I need a repair or a full replacement?+

A general rule: if the system is under 15 years old and the repair cost is less than half the replacement cost, repair usually makes sense. We'll walk you through both options honestly and let you decide.

How long does a furnace typically last?+

Most gas furnaces last 15–20 years with proper maintenance. Boilers can last 20–30 years. Annual tune-ups significantly extend equipment life and catch small problems before they become expensive ones.

Do you offer emergency heating service?+

Yes. We know that a heating failure in January isn't something that can wait. Call us at 609.296.3151 and we'll do our best to get to you as fast as possible.

What brands do you install and service?+

We are a certified Trane Comfort Specialist dealer and our preferred installation brand is Trane. For service and repair, we work on virtually all brands including Carrier, Lennox, Rheem, Weil-McLain, Burnham, and many more.

Do you provide free estimates?+

Yes — we provide free estimates on new system installations. For service calls and repairs, there is a diagnostic fee that is credited toward the cost of the repair.
