Is a Ductless Mini-Split Right for You?

Ductless systems are one of the most popular upgrades for Jersey Shore homeowners — especially for beach homes, additions, and spaces that are hard to cool with central air.

  • No ductwork required — ideal for older homes and additions
  • Heat and cool the same zones year-round
  • Energy-efficient — heat and cool only the rooms you use
  • Whisper-quiet operation
  • Wall-mounted or ceiling cassette options
  • Multi-zone systems for whole-home comfort
  • Great for shore houses and vacation rentals

Cooling Questions

How often should I service my air conditioner?+

Once a year, ideally in the spring before cooling season. Annual tune-ups keep your system efficient, extend its life, and catch problems before they become emergencies.

My AC is running but not cooling — what's wrong?+

Common causes include low refrigerant, a dirty filter or coil, a failing compressor, or electrical issues. Some are simple fixes; others require professional diagnosis. Call us and we'll sort it out.

How long does a central AC system last?+

Typically 12–18 years. Coastal properties can see shorter lifespans due to salt air — protective coatings and regular maintenance help maximize equipment life.

What size AC system do I need?+

Proper sizing requires a Manual J load calculation based on your home's size, insulation, windows, and layout. Bigger is not better — oversized units cycle on and off too quickly, leaving humidity high and comfort low.

Do you install ductless systems?+

Yes — ductless mini-splits are one of our most popular installations. They're perfect for additions, shore houses, and older homes without ductwork. Contact us for a free consultation.
